How to work out the number of reviews needed to lift your Google rating

Improving your Google rating comes down to maths. The rating on display is a weighted average of all your reviews. If you currently sit at 20 reviews with a 3.0 rating, you need around 40 five-star reviews to reach 4.3. With 50 existing reviews at 3.5, you'll need roughly 50 new ones to get to 4.2.
