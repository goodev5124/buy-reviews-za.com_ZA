What is Google My Business and why does it matter?

Google My Business (now called Google Business Profile) is Google's free tool for managing how your business shows up online. Your GMB listing carries your trading hours, address, photos and, above all, your customer reviews. It is the first thing South Africans see when they search for a local business. Buying Google My Business reviews turns that shopfront into a conversion machine.

How reviews shape your GMB listing ranking

Google's algorithm weighs three things for local ranking: relevance, distance and prominence. Google My Business reviews are the most powerful prominence signal there is. Google favours listings with plenty of recent reviews, a strong rating and owner replies. Every positive review tightens your grip on the local pack.

The Google local pack: how reviews put you there

The local pack (the 3 results shown alongside the Google Maps card) captures over 44% of clicks on local searches. To land there, your Google My Business listing needs a rating above 4 stars and a solid stack of reviews. Without them, you stand no chance against better-rated rivals.
